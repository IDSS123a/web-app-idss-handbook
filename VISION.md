# VISION.md — Why This Application Exists
# IDSS Handbook Web Application
# Version 1.0 — June 2026

---

## The Problem

P.U. Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo is a bilingual (Bosnian-German) private
primary school operating under two curriculum frameworks simultaneously: Kanton Sarajevo
educational law and German federal standards (Baden-Württemberg and Thuringia).

This dual-framework environment creates a large volume of institutional rules, procedures,
and pedagogical guidelines that every teacher must know and follow. This knowledge
currently exists as scattered documents — PDFs, DOCX files, printed manuals — with no
reliable way for teachers to:

- Find the current, approved version of any rule
- Know what changed when a document is updated
- Ask a question and get a reliable, institution-backed answer
- Prove they have read and understood the institutional guidelines

---

## The Solution

IDSS Handbook is a closed institutional knowledge platform that:

1. **Centralises** all institutional knowledge in one verified, always-current system
2. **Motivates** teachers to master that knowledge through a professional gamification system
3. **Answers** institutional questions via an AI chatbot grounded exclusively in approved documents
4. **Ensures** knowledge is current — every approved document update automatically refreshes
   all content: Handbook chapters, chatbot knowledge, and quiz questions

---

## Users

| Role | Who | Primary Need |
|---|---|---|
| Super Admin | Director (direktor@idss.ba) | Control, oversight, approval of all changes |
| Admin | Pedagog, Sekretar | Upload and prepare documents for approval |
| User | Teachers and employees | Read, learn, and verify institutional knowledge |

---

## Core Principles

**Verified knowledge only.** No content is generated from AI training data.
Every word in the Handbook, every chatbot answer, every quiz question
must be traceable to an approved USTAV document.

**Living ecosystem.** When the Director approves a document update,
the entire platform updates automatically — no manual rebuilding.

**Professional motivation.** The gamification system is serious and institutional.
It motivates teachers to engage with the material without being childish.

**Institutional trust.** Every user interaction is logged. The Director can see
everything. This is not surveillance — it is accountability and quality assurance.

---

## What This Is Not

- Not a public product
- Not a prototype or MVP to be replaced
- Not a content management system for public documents
- Not a general-purpose chatbot
- Not a replacement for official school communication channels

---

## Success Definition

The application is successful when:

1. Every IDSS teacher has completed 100% of the Handbook
2. The IDSS Asistent correctly answers institutional questions
   and gracefully declines questions outside its knowledge
3. When any school rule changes, the entire platform reflects the change
   within minutes of Director approval — automatically
4. The Director has full visibility into what every teacher knows
