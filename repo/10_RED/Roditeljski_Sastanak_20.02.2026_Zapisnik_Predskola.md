Ad 2.   
Od septembra do sada sva su se djeca u potpunosti adaptirala na strukturu i pravila predškolske grupe  . 
Početkom druge sedmice februara grupi se pridružio novi učenik, a djeca cu ga pozitivno prihvatila.   
 
Djeca se osjećaju sigurno i imaju svoje mjesto unutar socijalne strukture vršnjaka. Primijeti se i skok u 
emocionalnoj zrelosti, konflikti koji su u početku bili česti sada su isčezli. Djeca su se upoznala s 
konceptima i pisanjem brojeva od 1 do 10 te slov a engleksog alfabeta od A do K. Fond riječi na engleskom 
i njemačkom jeziku kod djece veći u odnosu na početak školske godine te da bolje razumiju složene 
rečenice na oba jezika. Djeca također sve više spontano koriste strane jezike te se primijeti veće 
samopouzdanje prilikom govora oba jezika.  
 
Prema zadacima pokazuju interes i pozitivan odnos. Generalno djeca pokazuju spremnost za pohađanje 
prvog razreda, emocionalno, kognitivno i socijalno.  
 
 
 
Ad 3.   
Dnevna struktura razreda ostaje ista kao i do sada. Usklađena je sa strukturom ostatka škole, naročito po 
pitanju korištenja dvorišta škole, obzirom da vodimo računa o tome da se predškolarci iz sigurnosnih 
razloga ne nalaze istovremeno u dvorištu s djecom  većih uzrasta. S dolaskom toplijeg vremena planiramo 
održati veći broj aktivnosti na otvorenom. Također imamo u planu aktivnosti obogatiti s više 
eksperimenata, više čitanja, zadataka za razvoj logičkog mišljenja. Također nastojimo pratiti dječije 
interes e i aktivnosti prilagoditi istim.  
 
 
 
 
 
 
Ad 4.   
Dolazak djece u učionicu je obavezan do 09:00h. Nakon zajedničkog doručka počinjemo sa predškolskim 
aktivnostima te je važno da djeca ne kasne. Obavezno je također izostanak javiti putem maila. U slučaju 
izostanka dužeg od tri dana usljed bolesti, neophodn o je dostaviti ljekarsko opravdanje.  
 
Istaknuto je da ukoliko dijete preuzima druga osoba da je obavezno istu informaciju dostaviti sekretarijatu 
Škole ili učiteljicama putem maila. Uz to postoji potreba za boljom komunikacijom sa roditeljima obzirom 
da povratna informacija roditelja često izostaje.  
 
 
 
Ad 5.

Ad 1.   
An activity was prepared for parents in which they were asked to reflect on their child’s abilities, interests, and 
capacities at the beginning of the school year and to compare them with their child as they are now. The aim of the 
activity was to highligh t the changes and progress in the child’s development over the past period.  
 
We discussed the parents’ observations verbally. In general, parents notice significant progress in their children and 
are satisfied with it. They observe a greater interest in activities such as writing, coloring, and cutting, as well as an 
increased inte rest in school and learning. They also highlight improved knowledge and understanding of the English 
and German languages.  
 
 
 
 
 
Ad 2.   
From September until now, all children have fully adapted to the structure and rules of the preschool group. At the 
beginning of the second week of February, a new student joined the group, and the children accepted him positively. 
The children feel safe a nd have found their place within the social structure of their peer group.  
 
A noticeable increase in emotional maturity can also be observed; conflicts that were frequent at the beginning have 
now largely disappeared. The children have become familiar with the concepts and writing of numbers from 1 to 10, 
as well as the letters of  the English alphabet from A to K.  
 
It was noted that the children’s vocabulary in English and German is broader compared to the beginning of the school 
year and that they better understand more complex sentences in both languages. The children are also increasingly 
using foreign languages s pontaneously, and greater confidence can be observed when speaking both languages.

MERGEFThe daily  classroom structure remains the same as before. It is aligned with the structure of the rest of the school, 
particularly regarding the use of the school yard, as we ensure for safety reasons that preschool children are not in 
the yard at the same time as o lder students.  
 
With the arrival of warmer weather, we plan to organize a greater number of outdoor activities. We also intend to 
enrich our program with more experiments, additional reading activities, and tasks aimed at developing logical 
thinking skills.  
 
At the same time, we strive to follow the children’s interests and adapt activities accordingly.  
 
 
 
 
Ad 4.   
Children’s arrival to the classroom is mandatory by 9:00 a.m. After a shared breakfast, we begin preschool activities, 
so it is important that children do not arrive late.  
 
It is also mandatory to inform the school about any absence via email. In the case of an absence longer than three 
days due to illness, a medical certificate must be provided.  
 
It was emphasized that if a child is picked up by another person, this information must be communicated to the 
School Secretariat or to the teachers via email.  
 
Additionally, there is a need for improved communication with parents, as feedback from parents is often lacking.  
. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Ad 5.   
It is planned that each month we will organize a field trip or visit in accordance with the current theme and the 
children’s interests. We are looking forward to visiting the National Museum of Bosnia and Herzegovina, followed 
by a visit to ZOO Bambi, as w ell as the planetarium.  
 
All communication regarding field trips, as well as payment for excursions, will from now on be handled exclusively 
with the teachers. Children whose parents do not complete the payment by the agreed deadline will not be able to 
attend the trip.
