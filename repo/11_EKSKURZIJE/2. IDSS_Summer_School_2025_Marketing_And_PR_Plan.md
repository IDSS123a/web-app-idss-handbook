**Marketing and PR Plan**

**Sarajevo, January 2026**

**✨ IDSS Summer School 2026 – "Spielen. Entdecken. Freunde finden." ✨**

**🎯 Key Message**

**An unforgettable bilingual (German-English) summer adventure in the heart of Sarajevo – where children play, discover, and make friends for life. No prior German knowledge required!**

**📢 MAIN PROMOTIONAL CAMPAIGN**

**Campaign Title**

**"Spielen. Entdecken. Freunde finden. – Your Child’s Summer Adventure Starts Here!"**

**🎨 Branding Slogan**

**"Spielen. Entdecken. Freunde finden."**

**🌍 Special Positioning for Foreign Presence**

- **Bilingual Program** : Activities conducted in **English and German** , with Bosnian/Croatian/Serbian language support available. **No prior German knowledge required!**
- **International Focus** : Students from embassies, international organizations, foreign companies, and NGOs are warmly welcomed.
- **Premium Care and Education** : IDSS upholds **European educational standards** with certified educators.
- **Excursions to Historic and Natural Wonders** : Sarajevo, Visoko, Konjic, Trebević, Baščaršija – children will discover Bosnia and Herzegovina like locals!

**📣 DISTRIBUTION STRATEGY**

**1. Direct Embassy Outreach**

- Personalized invitations and brochures sent to all embassies in Sarajevo (list appended)
- Include a **special welcome offer** for early registration by **June 15, 2026**
- Highlight: **No German knowledge required – all children welcome!**

**2. Corporate Packages**

- Propose partnerships with international companies:
    - Siemens BH, Raiffeisen Bank, KPMG, Deloitte, Al Jazeera Balkans, Coca-Cola HBC, and others
- Offer group registration benefits (5% discount for company employees' children)

**3. Digital Campaign**

- **Facebook and Instagram campaign** : Focus on Sarajevo-based expat groups and local families
- **Google Ads** : Keywords like "Sarajevo Summer Camp for Kids," "International Summer School Bosnia," "German Summer School Sarajevo," "bilingual summer camp"
- **LinkedIn Ads** : Targeting expats and international professionals in Bosnia and Herzegovina

**4. Web Application – Central Hub**

- **Primary registration channel:** [https://summer-school-2026.vercel.app/](https://summer-school-2026.vercel.app/)
- The website contains:
    - Full programme details
    - Daily schedule and excursion plan
    - Pricing and enrolment steps
    - Meet the team
    - Frequently Asked Questions
    - Online registration form

**5. On-Site Promotion**

- Host a **Mini Open Day** in **early June 2026** :
    - Invite embassy and company representatives with their families
    - Showcase facilities, meet staff, and experience a sample day of Summer School
    - Distribute printed materials with QR code linking to the web application

**🏆 Additional Premium Features to Highlight**

- **Personalized Certificates** of participation
- **Digital photo gallery** (private link for enrolled families only)
- **IDSS Summer School T-shirt** for every participant
- **Multicultural classroom setting** that fosters global citizenship
- **Final day event** : Parents invited to celebrate children's achievements
