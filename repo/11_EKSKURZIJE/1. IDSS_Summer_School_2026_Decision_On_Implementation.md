BASED ON ARTICLE 12 OF THE LAW ON INSTITUTIONS ("OFFICIAL GAZETTE OF FEDERATION BIH", NO. 6/92, 8/93 AND 13/94), ARTICLE 11 OF THE RULES OF P.U. INTERNATIONALE DEUTSCHE SCHULE SARAJEVO – MEĐUNARODNA NJEMAČKA ŠKOLA SARAJEVO NO: 01-1-4/2025 DATED JANUARY 27, 2025, THE SCHOOL BOARD AT ITS SESSION HELD ON [DATUM] ADOPTS THE FOLLOWING

**DECISION**

**ON IMPLEMENTATION OF SUMMER SCHOOL 2026**

**Article 1**

**(Implementation Authorization)**

(1) This Decision authorizes the implementation of the Summer School program at P.U. Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo – Međunarodna Njemačka Škola Sarajevo for the period from **August 3, 2026, to August 14, 2026** .

**Article 2**

**(Program Framework)**

(1) The Summer School program is organized under the motto: **"Spielen. Entdecken. Freunde finden."**

(2) The program is bilingual (English + German), with support in Bosnian, Croatian, and Serbian. No prior knowledge of German is required for participation.

(3) The program shall be implemented in accordance with the Plan and Program for Summer School 2026, which forms an integral part of this Decision.

**Article 3**

**(Staff Engagement)**

(1) The School Director is authorized to engage the following staff for program implementation:

| a)   | Programme Coordinator – Davor Mulalić, Director of IDSS          |
|------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| b)   | Head of Summer School – Mubera Ademović                          |
| c)   | Teaching Assistant (Junior Group, ages 6–9) – Selma Mujanović    |
| d)   | Teaching Assistant (Mittel Group, ages 9–12) – to be determined  |
| e)   | Teaching Assistant (Senior Group, ages 12–15) – to be determined |
| f)   | Facility Manager – Benjamin Halimić                              |
| g)   | Cleaning Staff – Đenana Čizmo and Maja Bajraktarević             |

(2) All engaged staff shall sign employment contracts no later than April 22, 2026.

**Article 4**

**(Age Groups and Enrollment)**

(1) The Summer School program is organized into three age groups:

| a)   | Gruppe Junior   | ages 6–9 years   |
|------|-----------------|------------------|
| b)   | Gruppe Mittel   | ages 9–12 years  |
| c)   | Gruppe Senior   | ages 12–15 years |

(2) The maximum number of participants is **25 children** .

(3) The minimum number of enrolled students required for program implementation is **20 children** .

(4) If by July 20, 2026, fewer than 15 children are enrolled, the School Director may recommend cancellation of the program to the School Board.

**Article 5**

**(Financial Framework)**

(1) The tuition fees are set as follows:

| a)   | Full programme (2 weeks, August 3–14, 2026)      | **500,00 KM**   |
|------|--------------------------------------------------|-----------------|
| b)   | One week only (August 3–7 or August 10–14, 2026) | **300,00 KM**   |

(2) The fee includes:

|    |                                                   |
|----|---------------------------------------------------|
| a) | Educational program and all planned activities    |
| b) | All teaching and creative materials               |
| c) | Daily breakfast, lunch, and snack                 |
| d) | All excursions, entrance fees, and transportation |
| e) | Accident insurance for all participants           |
| f) | IDSS Summer School 2026 T-shirt                   |
| g) | Personalized certificate of participation         |

(3) Payment shall be made within **5 business days** of signing the enrollment contract.

(4) The financial plan for the Summer School program is an integral part of this Decision.

**Article 6**

**(Cancellation Policy)**

(1) In the event of cancellation by the parent:

| a)   | 15 or more days before the start of the program (by July 19, 2026)   | 50% refund upon written request   |
|------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| b)   | Less than 15 days before the start of the program                    | No refund                         |
| c)   | After the program has commenced                                      | No refund                         |

(2) In cases of force majeure (illness with medical documentation), refunds shall be considered individually by the School Director.

**Article 7**

**(Excursion Plan)**

(1) The program includes the following excursions:

**Week 1 (August 3–7, 2026)**

| a)   | August 4, 2026   | ZOO Bambi, Hrasnica                            |
|------|------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| b)   | August 5, 2026   | Baščaršija and Old Town Sarajevo (guided tour) |
| c)   | August 6, 2026   | Trebević Mountain and Cable Car                |

**Week 2 (August 10–14, 2026)**

| d)   | August 10, 2026   | Pyramid of the Sun, Visoko     |
|------|-------------------|--------------------------------|
| e)   | August 11, 2026   | Klas d.d. Factory Tour         |
| f)   | August 13, 2026   | Konjic and Titov Bunker ARK D0 |

(2) Transportation shall be provided by licensed partner carriers.

(3) All excursions include appropriate supervision, first aid equipment, and emergency protocols.

**Article 8**

**(Space and Resources)**

(1) The program will utilize:

| a)   | 4 classrooms            |
|------|-------------------------|
| b)   | School yard             |
| c)   | Art room                |
| d)   | Library                 |
| f)   | Kitchen and dining area |

**Article 9**

**(Safety and Health)**

(1) The School Director is responsible for:

| a)   | Continuous supervision of children from 08:00 to 17:00 daily                        |
|------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| b)   | Ensuring all staff hold valid first aid certification                               |
| c)   | Maintaining complete emergency contact lists                                        |
| d)   | Securing accident insurance for all participants                                    |
| e)   | Implementing headcount protocols for all excursions                                 |
| f)   | Collecting detailed health and allergy information from parents prior to enrollment |

**Article 10**

**(Implementation Responsibility)**

(1) The School Director is responsible for:

| a)   | Program implementation in accordance with this Decision   |
|------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| b)   | Staff engagement and supervision                          |
| c)   | Resource allocation                                       |
| d)   | Safety measures and emergency protocols                   |
| e)   | Final financial reconciliation and program evaluation     |

**Article 11**

**(Reporting)**

(1) The School Director shall submit to the School Owner:

|    |                                                                                |
|----|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| a) | A preliminary enrollment report by July 25, 2026                               |
| b) | A final program report, including financial reconciliation, by August 31, 2026 |
| c) | Parent and participant feedback summary by September 15, 2026                  |

**Article 12**

**(Entry into Force)**

(1) This Decision enters into force on the day of its adoption.

(2) The Decision shall be displayed on the school's notice board and published on the school's website.&gt;
