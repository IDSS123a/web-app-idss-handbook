Based on Article 12 of the Law on Institutions ("Official Gazette of Federation BiH", No. 6/92, 8/93 and 13/94), Article 11 of the Rules of P.U. Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo - International German School Sarajevo No: 01-1-4/2025 dated January 27, 2025, the School Board at its session held on [DATUM] adopts the following

**RULES AND REGULATIONS**

**FOR SUMMER SCHOOL 2026**

**Article 1**

**(Purpose and Objectives)**

The Summer School is established to contribute to the strategic goals of the International German School Sarajevo (hereinafter: IDSS). Its primary purposes are to enhance the school's reputation within Bosnia and Herzegovina and internationally, advance the quality of educational programming through innovative approaches, and provide children with enriching summer experiences that complement their regular academic year.

The program is organized under the motto:

"Spielen. Entdecken. Freunde finden."

**Article 2**

**(Educational Philosophy and Benefits)**

(1) The Summer School, as an integral part of IDSS, provides a comprehensive development platform that:

| a) | Facilitates social growth through exposure to diverse peer groups and cultural exchanges |

| b) | Creates opportunities for authentic cultural and linguistic development in a bilingual environment (English + German) |

| c) | Develops independence and self-confidence through structured activities and guided exploration |

| d) | Encourages personal growth outside traditional learning environments |

| e) | Fosters talent discovery and skill development through varied programming |

| f) | Promotes international mindedness and cultural understanding |

| g) | Builds resilience through new challenges and experiences |

| h) | Enables natural language acquisition through play, songs, and activities – no prior German knowledge required |

**Article 3**

**(Program Structure and Eligibility)**

(1) Age Groups: The program serves children aged 6 to 15 years, organized into three age-appropriate groups:

| Group | Age Range | Focus |

|-----------|---------------|-----------|

| Gruppe Junior | 6–9 years | Play, creativity, basic German through stories and songs |

| Gruppe Mittel | 9–12 years | Language activities, sports, team projects |

| Gruppe Senior | 12–15 years | Debate, presentations, cultural projects, leadership |

(2) Eligibility: The program is open to:

| a) | Current IDSS students |

| b) | Students from other schools in Sarajevo Canton |

| c) | International students |

| d) | Children from the broader community |

(3) Maximum number of participants: 25 children

(4) Minimum number of participants required for implementation: 20 children

(5) Leadership Structure:

| a) | Programme Coordinator – Davor Mulalić, Director of IDSS |

| b) | Head of Summer School – Mubera Ademović |

| c) | Teaching Assistants (3) – one per age group |

| d) | Facility Manager |

| e) | Support staff |

**Article 4**

**(Communication and Information Dissemination)**

Program information is disseminated through multiple channels to ensure broad accessibility:

(1) Digital Platforms:

| a) | IDSS official website |

| b) | Dedicated web application: [https://summer-school-2026.vercel.app/](https://summer-school-2026.vercel.app/) |

| c) | Social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram) |

| d) | Electronic newsletters |

(2) Physical Communications:

| a) | School bulletin boards |

| b) | Printed brochures |

| c) | Information packets |

(3) Direct Communication:

| a) | Parent information sessions |

| b) | Email updates |

| c) | Phone consultations |

| d) | WhatsApp group for enrolled families |

**Article 5**

**(Program Implementation and Content)**

(1) Languages of Instruction:

| a) | English (primary language of instruction) |

| b) | German (introduced naturally through play, songs, and activities) |

| c) | Bosnian/Croatian/Serbian (support as needed) |

Important: No prior knowledge of German is required for participation.

(2) Program Development Process:

| a) | Initial design by Head of Summer School |

| b) | Review and input from teaching staff |

| c) | Principal oversight and guidance |

| d) | Final approval by School Board |

(3) Required Program Elements:

| a) | Clear learning objectives for each activity |

| b) | Detailed daily and weekly schedules |

| c) | Comprehensive staffing plan |

| d) | Risk assessment and management procedures |

| e) | Minimum and maximum participant thresholds |

| f) | Assessment and evaluation methods |

| g) | Excursion plan with all locations and contacts |

**Article 6**

**(Schedule and Financial Information)**

(1) Program Duration: August 3–14, 2026 (two weeks)

(2) Daily Schedule:

| a) | Monday through Friday |

| b) | Hours: 08:00 – 17:00 |

| c) | Structured activities, excursions, and free play periods |

(3) Attendance Options and Fees:

| a) | Full two-week program (August 3–14, 2026): 500 BAM |

| b) | One-week option (August 3–7 OR August 10–14, 2026): 300 BAM |

(4) Included in the fee:

| a) | Educational program and all planned activities |

| b) | All teaching and creative materials |

| c) | Daily breakfast, lunch, and snack |

| d) | All excursions, entrance fees, and transportation |

| e) | Accident insurance for all participants |

| f) | IDSS Summer School 2026 T-shirt |

| g) | Personalized certificate of participation |

(5) Registration Process:

| a) | Registration opens: April 15, 2026 |

| b) | Final Deadline: July 25, 2026 |

| c) | Online application: [https://summer-school-2026.vercel.app/](https://summer-school-2026.vercel.app/) |

| d) | Payment due within 5 working days of registration |

| e) | Enrollment confirmed only upon receipt of: |

| i) | Signed contract (two copies) |

| ii) | Full payment |

| iii) | Completed medical information form |

| iv) | Signed parental consent (including photography consent) |

| v) | Emergency contact details (minimum 2 contacts) |

**Article 7**

**(Refund and Cancellation Policy)**

(1) 50% refund available only under the following conditions:

| a) | Written withdrawal notice provided at least 15 days before program start (by July 19, 2026) |

| b) | Documented justification (medical, family emergency, etc.) |

| c) | Submission of required cancellation forms |

(2) No refunds available:

| a) | Less than 15 days before program start |

| b) | After program commencement |

| c) | For partial attendance |

(3) Force Majeure Provisions:

| a) | Special considerations for extraordinary circumstances (illness with medical documentation) |

| b) | Individual review by the School Director |

**Article 8**

**(Excursion Plan)**

(1) The program includes the following guided excursions:

Week 1 (August 3–7, 2026)

| a) | August 4, 2026 | ZOO Bambi, Hrasnica |

| b) | August 5, 2026 | Baščaršija and Old Town Sarajevo (guided tour) |

| c) | August 6, 2026 | Trebević Mountain and Cable Car |

Week 2 (August 10–14, 2026)

| d) | August 10, 2026 | Pyramid of the Sun, Visoko |

| e) | August 11, 2026 | Klas d.d. Factory Tour |

| f) | August 13, 2026 | Konjic and Titov Bunker ARK D0 |

(2) Transportation shall be provided by licensed partner carriers.

(3) All excursions include appropriate supervision, first aid equipment, and emergency protocols.

(4) On excursion days, meals shall be provided as packed lunches or at partner restaurants.

**Article 10**

**(Safety and Health Measures)**

(1) IDSS ensures:

| a) | Continuous supervision of children from 08:00 to 17:00 daily |

| b) | First aid equipment and trained staff (all staff hold valid first aid certification) |

| c) | Emergency procedures protocol |

| d) | Compliance with health and safety regulations |

(2) Parents must declare all food allergies and special medical conditions on the enrollment form.

**(Quality Assurance and Monitoring)**

(1) Attendance and Participation Monitoring:

| b) | Activity participation tracking |

| c) | Progress notes for each participant |

(2) Program Evaluation Through:

| a) | Regular staff feedback sessions |

| b) | Parent communication and post-program surveys |

| c) | Child satisfaction feedback (age-appropriate format) |

| d) | Head of Summer School oversight |

| e) | End-of-program evaluation report submitted to School Director |

**(Certification and Recognition)**

(1) All participants receive:

| a) | Official IDSS Summer School 2026 completion certificate (personalized) |

| c) | IDSS Summer School 2026 silicone wristband |

(2) Recognition of special achievements or contributions.

**(Communication with Parents)**

| a) | Enrollment confirmation (email immediately upon payment) |

(3) After the program:

| a) | Post-program satisfaction survey (Google Forms) |

| b) | Link to private digital photo gallery (within 14 days) |

**Article 14**

**(Implementation and Review)**

(1) This Rulebook enters into force upon adoption.

(2) Annual review and updates as needed.

(3) Amendments possible with appropriate approval.
