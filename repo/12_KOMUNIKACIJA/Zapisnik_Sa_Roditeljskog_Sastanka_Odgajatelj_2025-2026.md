**Upute za vođenje zapisnika**

Zapisnik ima obilježje isprave kojom se potvrđuje rad i određuje oblik i tok sastanka!

Zapisnik obavezno mora sadržavati:

1. **Naziv sastanka** ( *s rednim brojem* ), *npr. Zapisnik s I roditeljskog sastanka u [kolskoj 2024/2025 godini*
2. **Mjesto** *održavanja sastanka*
3. **Datum** *održavanja sastanka*
4. **Vrijeme** *početka održavanja sastanka* ( *u satima* )
5. **Broj i imena prisutnih** ( *imena i prezimena roditelja i zaposlenika* )
6. **Broj i imena odsutnih** ( *imena i prezimena odsutnih, te opravdano ili neopravdano* )
7. **Popis osoba koje prisustvuju sastanku** ( *mogu učestvovati u radu ali su bez prava pri glasanju* )
8. **Utvrđivanje kvoruma** ( *potvrda da je na sastanku prisutan potreban broj članova za pravovaljano odlučivanje* )
9. **Ime predsjedavajućeg** *na sastanku* ( *nije potrebno ako je propisano nekim aktom škole* )
10. **Ime zapisničara** *na sastanku*
11. **Dnevni red** – *sastanak se održava po točkama dnevnog reda*
12. Predsjedavajući na sastanku **PREDLAŽE DNEVNI RED** i daje ga na usvajanje prisutnim koji ga glasanjem prihvataju – A MOGU GA I NADOPUNITI
13. **Zapis rada sastanka** :

- **tok rada** *sastanka i predmete o kojima se raspravljalo, te imena osoba koje su učestvovale u raspravi i sažet prikaz njihovog izlaganja*
- **rezultati glasanja** o pojedinim prijedlozima odnosno točkama dnevnog reda
- **izdvojeno mišljenje pojedinog člana** , ako on zatraži da se to unese u zapisnik

1. **Vrijeme završetka** ili prekida sastanka
2. **Oznaku priloga** *koji su sastavni dio zapisnika* (ako ih ima)
3. Potpis predsjedavajućeg na sastanku i zapisničara

Tokom sastanka:

- *predsjedavajući na sastanku obrazlaže temu/prijedlog*
- *otvara se rasprava o temi*
- *donosi se odluka – glasanjem* *(može biti javno ili tajno)*
- *za donošenja odluke predsjedavajući na sastaku pita* ***ko je ZA, ko je PROTIV,*** *te* *ko je SUZDRŽAN*
- *način tajnog glasanja je propisan aktom škole*
- *Zapisničar bilježi sve pod imenom i prezimenom diskutanta u prvom licu*
- *Prepričavanjem*

Tok sastanka se može bilježiti:

*Ukoliko je neko od prisutnih napustio sastanak ili je neko naknadno došao, zapisničar to mora navesti u zapisniku (zabilježiti ime i prezime te vrijeme odlaska/dolaska)*

**Guidelines for taking minutes**

Minutes have the characteristic of a document that confirms the work and determines the form and course of the meeting!

**Minutes must contain:**

1. **Title of the meeting** (with ordinal number), e.g., Minutes from the 1st parent-teacher meeting in the 2024/2025 school year
2. **Location** of the meeting
3. **Date** of the meeting
4. **Start time** of the meeting (in hours)
5. **Number and names of those present** (first and last names of parents and employees)
6. **Number and names of those absent** (first and last names of those absent, and whether excused or unexcused)
7. **List of persons attending the meeting** (who can participate in the work but without voting rights)
8. **Verification of quorum** (confirmation that the required number of members for valid decision-making is present at the meeting)
9. **Name of the chairperson** at the meeting (not necessary if prescribed by a school act)
10. **Name of the secretary** at the meeting
11. **Agenda** – the meeting is held according to agenda items
12. **The chairperson** at the meeting PROPOSES THE AGENDA and submits it for approval to those present who accept it by voting – AND THEY CAN ALSO SUPPLEMENT IT
13. **Record of the meeting work** :

- **course of the meeting** and matters discussed, as well as the names of persons who participated in the discussion and a concise presentation of their statements
- **voting results** on individual proposals or agenda items
- **separate opinion of an individual member,** if they request it to be included in the minutes

1. **Time of completion or interruption** of the meeting
2. **Indication of attachments** that are an integral part of the minutes (if any)
3. **Signature of the chairperson** at the meeting and the secretary

**During the meeting:**

- the chairperson at the meeting explains the topic/proposal
- discussion on the topic is opened
- a decision is made – by voting (can be public or secret)
- for decision-making, the chairperson at the meeting asks **who is FOR** , **who is AGAINST** , and **who ABSTAINS**
- the method of secret voting is prescribed by the school act

**The course of the meeting can be recorded:**
