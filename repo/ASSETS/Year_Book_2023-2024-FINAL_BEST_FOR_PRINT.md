Dear Students, Parents, and Friends of IDSS!
Welcome to the 2024 edition of the IDSS Elementary School Yearbook! 
This yearbook is a celebration of our vibrant school community and the
memorable moments we've shared throughout the year. As you turn
each page, you'll find snapshots of our students' achievements,
creativity, and the friendships that make our school such a special place. 
2024 has been a year of growth and learning, filled with exciting events,
inspiring lessons, and joyful experiences. From the first day of school to
the many activities, projects, and events, our students have
demonstrated remarkable dedication, curiosity, and enthusiasm. This
yearbook captures those moments, preserving them for years to come. 
We extend our heartfelt thanks to the teachers and staff who work
tirelessly to create a nurturing and stimulating environment for our
students. Your passion and commitment are the foundations of our
school’s success. 
To our wonderful students, this yearbook is for you. It is a reminder of
your hard work, your laughter, and the memories you have created with
your friends. As you look through these pages, remember the fun times,
the challenges you overcame, and the milestones you reached. 
A special thank you goes to the yearbook committee for their incredible
effort in compiling and designing this beautiful book. Your dedication
and creativity have made this yearbook a true reflection of the spirit of
IDSS Elementary School. 
We hope you enjoy looking back on this year and that this yearbook
serves as a cherished keepsake of your time at IDSS Elementary School. 
 With warmest regards,
 Principal IDSS Elementary School 
Davor Mulalic

THE 1ST DAY OF SCHOOL
OUR ANNUAL FIRST CLASS ENROLLMENT EVENT IS A
HEARTWARMING TRADITION AT OUR ELEMENTARY SCHOOL.
EACH NEW STUDENT MARKS THIS SPECIAL MILESTONE BY
LIFTING UP A BALLOON WITH A PERSONAL WISH ATTACHED,
SYMBOLIZING THEIR HOPES AND DREAMS FOR THE FUTURE.
THIS CEREMONY NOT ONLY WELCOMES OUR YOUNGEST
LEARNERS BUT ALSO FOSTERS A SENSE OF COMMUNITY AND
OPTIMISM AS WE CELEBRATE THE BEGINNING OF THEIR
EDUCATIONAL JOURNEY.

TRADITION!


OUR ANNUAL WINTER CELEBRATION IS A CHERISHED EVENT
AT OUR ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, BRINGING TOGETHER
STUDENTS, PARENTS, AND TEACHERS TO SHARE IN THE JOY

OUR ANNUAL PROJECT WEEK IS A HIGHLIGHT AT OUR ELEMENTARY
SCHOOL, OFFERING STUDENTS A CHANCE TO DIVE DEEP INTO
CREATIVE AND ENGAGING TOPICS BEYOND THE REGULAR
CURRICULUM. THIS IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCE FOSTERS TEAMWORK,
CURIOSITY, AND HANDS-ON LEARNING, CULMINATING IN
PRESENTATIONS THAT SHOWCASE THE STUDENTS' HARD WORK
AND ENTHUSIASM. 
DURING THIS EXCITING WEEK, THE SCHOOL OPENS ITS DOORS TO
PARENTS AND THE COMMUNITY, ALLOWING THEM TO WITNESS
OUR STUDENTS' CREATIVITY AND HARD WORK FIRSTHAND.
THIS YEAR’S TOPIC: “THE OLYMPIC GAMES”


OUR ANNUAL READING DAY COMPETITION IS A BELOVED
TRADITION AT OUR ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, DESIGNED TO INSPIRE A
LOVE OF READING AND CELEBRATE LITERARY SKILLS. STUDENTS
PARTICIPATE IN A VARIETY OF READING ACTIVITIES AND CONTESTS,
SHOWCASING THEIR COMPREHENSION AND CREATIVITY. IT'S A FUN
AND ENRICHING DAY THAT HIGHLIGHTS THE JOY AND IMPORTANCE
OF READING.


OUR ANNUAL SPORTS DAY COMPETITION IS A THRILLING
EVENT AT OUR ELEMENTARY SCHOOL, WHERE STUDENTS
COMPETE IN A VARIETY OF ATHLETIC ACTIVITIES. IT'S A DAY
FULL OF EXCITEMENT, TEAMWORK, AND SCHOOL SPIRIT,
PROMOTING PHYSICAL FITNESS AND CAMARADERIE
AMONG STUDENTS. EVERYONE LOOKS FORWARD TO THIS
ENERGETIC CELEBRATION OF SPORTSMANSHIP AND FUN.
