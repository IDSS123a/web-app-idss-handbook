Subject: Laternenfest  
 
Dear Parents/Guardians,  
We are delighted to invite you to join us in celebrating Laternenfest, which will be held 
on Monda y, November 10, 2024. Preschoolers and students from grades  I to IV will walk 
with lanterns to the Great Park, where we will sing songs together and listen to stories 
about this date.  
Upon returning to the school, we will light a campfire and continue our gathering with 
refreshments until 6:00 PM.  
Please plan to arrive at the school by 4:40 PM.  
We look forward to our gathering and spending time together!  
If you have any questions or need additional information, please feel free to contact 
us at info@idss.ba or +387 33 560 520.
