Procedure for Equivalence/Recognition of Foreign Certificates for 
Enrolling a Child in “Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo ” 
 
 
To enroll a child in our elementary school with a foreign certificate, you need to complete 
the equivalence/recognition process through the Ministry of Education, Science, and Youth 
of the Sarajevo Canton. Below are the steps you need to follow:  
 
 
Step 1: Prepare the Required Documents  
 
Ensure that you have the following documents ready:  
 
1. Application Form:  Complete the application form for the equivalence/recognition of the 
certificate, which can be obtained from the Ministry's website or in person at the Ministry's 
office.  
 
2. Original Certificate and Transcript : Provide the original certificate or diploma and 
transcripts from the previous school attended abroad.  
 
3. Certified Translation : Obtain a certified translation of the certificate and transcripts into 
one of the official languages of Bosnia and Herzegovina (Bosnian, Croatian, or Serbian). 
The translation must be done by a court -certified translator.  
 
4. Curriculum Overview : Include a curriculum overview from the previous school, detailing 
the subjects studied and the duration of each subject.  
 
5. Proof of Legalization : Ensure that the original certificate has been legalized in the country 
of origin (Apostille or other form of legalization depending on the country).  
 
6. Passport Copy : Provide a copy of the child’s passport or birth certificate.  
 
7. Receipt of Payment : Include proof of payment of the administrative fee for the 
equivalence/recognition process.  
 
 
Step 2: Submit the Application  
 
Submit all required documents to the Department for Education, Science, Culture, and 
Sports of the Sarajevo Canton at their office or via their official mailing address. Make sure 
to submit original documents along with copies.  
 
 
Step 3: Verification and Decision  
 
The Ministry will review and verify the submitted documents. This process may involve 
comparing the foreign certificate with the local education standards.  
 
You may be asked to provide additional information or documents if needed.

Step 4: Issuance of the Equivalence Decision  
 
Once the verification is completed, the Ministry will issue a decision on the 
equivalence/recognition of the foreign certificate. This decision will specify the equivalency 
of the foreign certificate to the local education standards.  
 
You will receive a formal document confirming the equivalence, which will allow the child 
to enroll in the corresponding grade in our elementary school.  
 
Step 5: Enrollment in School  
 
Submit the equivalence decision, along with other enrollment documents, to our 
elementary school administration to complete the enrollment process.  
 
Important Notes:  
 
The entire equivalence process may take several weeks, so it is recommended to start early 
before the school year begins.  
   
Keep copies of all submitted documents for your records.  
 
Ensure that all steps are followed as per the guidelines from the Ministry of Education, 
Science, and Youth of the Sarajevo Canton to avoid delays.
