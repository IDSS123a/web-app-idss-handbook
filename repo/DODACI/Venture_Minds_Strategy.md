Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo

## Venture Minds

Strategic Document, v1.0

*One-cycle pilot strategy  ·  Academic year 2026–27*

1. STRATEGIC CONTEXT

**Why Venture Minds, and why now**

Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo (IDSS) operates in a market that demands more than academic excellence. Parents who choose an international private school are investing in a specific outcome: a child who thinks clearly, communicates confidently, and understands the world they are entering. Venture Minds is IDSS structured response to that demand, a program that extends the school's core philosophy beyond the standard curriculum.

The timing is deliberate. The pilot is designed to run within a single school year, at a scale that is fully manageable, financially self-sustaining, and measurable. If the pilot succeeds, it becomes the foundation for a permanent program embedded in the IDSS identity.

|    | *Venture Minds is not an extracurricular activity. It is an extension of what*  IDSS  *already stands for, delivered in a new format, for students ready to go further.*   |
|----|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

2. STRATEGIC POSITION

**How Venture Minds sits within IDSS**

Venture Minds is positioned as a visible, branded program that carries the IDSS name and reflects its pedagogical standards. It is not an outsourced activity or a third-party course running inside the school. It is an IDSS program, designed by IDSS leadership, delivered to IDSS students, and accountable to IDSS quality standards.

| **Type**               | Paid enrichment program, one cycle per academic year     |
|------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| **Language**           | English, exclusively                                     |
| **Target group**       | Grades 7–9 (ages 12–14), IDSS students only              |
| **Brand relationship** | Venture Minds by Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo |
| **Future pathway**     | Potential integration into IDSS curriculum development   |
| **Exclusivity**        | Closed to external students, IDSS only                   |

3. STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES

**Three dimensions of success**

Success in the pilot cycle is defined across three dimensions. All three must be met for the program to be considered successful and eligible for continuation.

| **F**   | **Financial sustainability**  The program covers all costs and generates a positive net result for the school without external funding.   |
|---------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

| **R**   | **Reputational impact**  Parents and students speak positively about the program. At least one tangible word-of-mouth or community signal is documented.   |
|---------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

| **P**   | **Pedagogical outcome**  Students demonstrate a measurable shift in how they engage with ideas, asking better questions, making connections across subjects, and communicating with greater clarity.   |
|---------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

**Measurable targets for the pilot cycle**

| **Dimension**   | **Target**                                                    | **Measurement method**            |
|-----------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Financial       | Net positive result (min. +500 KM)                            | Post-cycle financial summary      |
| Financial       | Minimum 11 students enrolled at start                         | Enrollment register               |
| Financial       | Zero unpaid invoices at cycle end                             | Payment tracking sheet            |
| Reputational    | 80%+ parent satisfaction (informal survey)                    | End-of-cycle parent questionnaire |
| Reputational    | At least 5 families express interest in next cycle            | Director's record                 |
| Reputational    | Demo Day attended by at least 1 external guest                | Attendance register               |
| Pedagogical     | 100% of students complete the cycle                           | Attendance log                    |
| Pedagogical     | Every student presents at Demo Day                            | Demo Day record                   |
| Pedagogical     | Instructor documents one cross-subject connection per student | Session notes                     |

4. STRATEGIC PILLARS

**Four pillars that carry the strategy**

**Pillar 1, Quality of delivery**

The program lives or dies by what happens in the room. A strong concept and a solid financial model become irrelevant if the sessions are weak. Quality of delivery is therefore the first and highest-priority pillar.

- The instructor is selected through a structured process including a trial session with real students.
- Every session follows the governing principle: minimum 70% active work, maximum 15 minutes of theory.
- The instructor submits brief session notes after each class, one page, focused on what worked and what did not.
- The principal reviews notes monthly and intervenes if standards are not met.
- All communication to parents is signed by the principal and uses IDSS letterhead.
- Student materials carry the Venture Minds by Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo mark.
- The Demo Day is a formal school event, not an informal presentation.
- Certificates are signed by the principal and carry the school seal.
- Enrollment is confirmed only upon receipt of signed contract and first payment.
- Instructor is paid monthly, on a fixed date, via formal contract.
- A simple one-page financial tracker is updated after each payment cycle.
- No cycle begins without a minimum of 11 enrolled students.
- The instructor documents real-world connections made during each session (to math, language, science).
- At the end of the cycle, the principal produces a one-page synthesis: what Venture Minds revealed about student thinking.
- This synthesis is the input for any future curriculum development conversation.
- Enrollment contract binds families to the full cycle. Early exit without medical documentation does not trigger a refund.
- A waitlist of minimum 3 students is maintained from day one. If a place becomes available in the first two weeks, it is filled immediately.
- Instructor payment is fixed regardless of attendance fluctuations. This cost is treated as non-negotiable.
- No program launch without confirmed enrollment of at least 11 students and signed instructor contract.
- One cycle. One group. Done properly.
- Financially self-sustaining from day one. No external funding required.
- Visibly and unambiguously an IDSS program, in language, identity, and quality.
- Three measurable success criteria. If met, the program continues. If not, it is redesigned.
- The pilot generates the evidence for future curriculum integration, this is its deeper purpose.

**Pillar 2, IDSS brand integration**

Venture Minds must be identifiably IDSS. Parents should feel that this program could not exist in any other school in Sarajevo. Every touchpoint, from the enrollment letter to the Demo Day, carries the IDSS identity.

**Pillar 3, Financial discipline**

The program must be financially clean from day one. No informal arrangements, no deferred payments, no exceptions that set bad precedents. Financial discipline protects the school, the instructor, and the families.

**Pillar 4, Pathway to curriculum integration**

The pilot is not just a revenue experiment. It is a proof of concept for a new pedagogical layer at IDSS, one that, if successful, could inform how subjects are taught, how students are assessed, and how the school differentiates itself in the years ahead.

6. COMMUNICATION STRATEGY

**How we present Venture Minds to parents**

The program must be sold as something genuinely different, not as an expensive after-school activity, but as a structured, prestigious, English-language program that extends the IDSS academic experience. The language used in all parent communication must reflect this.

| **Do not say**                     | Do say                                                               |
|------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **"After-school club"**            | "Enrichment program"                                                 |
| **"Business course"**              | "Critical thinking through business"                                 |
| **"Extra classes"**                | "A separate program track"                                           |
| **"Certificate of participation"** | "Venture Minds Certificate, Internationale Deutsche Schule Sarajevo" |
| **"Taught by an instructor"**      | "Led by a practitioner with real-world experience"                   |

The Demo Day is the single most powerful communication tool available. One well-executed Demo Day, where students present confidently in English before parents and guests, generates more enrollment interest for the next cycle than any written communication. It is treated as a formal school event.

7. RISK STRATEGY

**Known risks and mitigations**

| **Risk**                                  | **Probability**   | **Mitigation**                                                                     |
|-------------------------------------------|-------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Weak instructor, poor delivery            | Medium            | Mandatory trial session before contract. Monthly review of session notes.          |
| Insufficient enrollment (< 11)            | Low-Medium        | Enrollment drive starts 6 weeks before program begins. No launch without minimum.  |
| Student dropout mid-cycle                 | Low               | Contract binds families. Waitlist fills vacancies in first 2 weeks.                |
| Program perceived as "just another class" | Medium            | Strong Demo Day. Consistent use of IDS brand. English-only delivery.               |
| Instructor unavailability (illness etc.)  | Low               | 48-hour notice clause in contract. Maximum 1 session may be rescheduled per cycle. |
| Parent dissatisfaction                    | Low               | Mid-cycle informal check-in (week 6). Principal available for any concerns.        |

8. FUTURE PATHWAY

**From pilot to permanent**

The one-cycle pilot is a proof of concept. If all three success dimensions are met, financial, reputational, and pedagogical, the program enters a structured development pathway.

| **Phase**                         | Action                                                                              |
|-----------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **After Cycle 1 (if successful)** | Launch Cycle 2 in the same academic year or next year opening                       |
| **After Cycle 2**                 | Evaluate expansion: second group, advanced level, or wider age range                |
| **Medium term**                   | Integrate Venture Minds thinking into selected IDSS subject areas                   |
| **Long term**                     | Venture Minds becomes a formal IDSS program, part of school identity and prospectus |
| **Funding (only after Cycle 1)**  | Apply for sponsorship: banks, IT companies, foundations                             |
| **Funding rule**                  | No external funding is sought before the first successful cycle                     |

|    | *Curriculum integration is not a goal for the pilot. It is the destination. The pilot generates the evidence that makes integration possible.*   |
|----|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
